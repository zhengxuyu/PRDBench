## Analysis Conclusions

Through in-depth analysis of golf tourist consumer behavior data, we found:

- Consumers exhibit different preference patterns in terms of price sensitivity, satisfaction, and amenities importance
- Consumers of different age groups and genders show significant differences in venue selection and consumption frequency
- There is a clear correlation between consumption behavior and personal characteristics, providing data support for precision marketing

## Marketing Recommendations

- Develop differentiated product and pricing strategies for different consumer groups
- Strengthen high-end facility development and improve overall service quality to meet consumer demands
- Develop personalized service plans to improve customer satisfaction and loyalty
