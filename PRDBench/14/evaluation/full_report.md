## Analysis Conclusions

Through in-depth analysis of golf tourist consumer behavior data, we found that:

- Consumers show different preference patterns in terms of price sensitivity, satisfaction and amenities importance
- Consumers of different age groups and genders have significant differences in venue selection and consumption frequency
- There is a clear correlation between consumption behavior and personal characteristics, providing data support for precision marketing

## Marketing Recommendations

- Develop differentiated product strategies and pricing strategies for different consumer groups
- Strengthen high-end facility construction to improve overall service quality to meet consumer needs
- Develop personalized service plans to improve customer satisfaction and loyalty
